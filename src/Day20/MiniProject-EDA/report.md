# Executive Report

This report highlights the top three findings from the EDA on the customer analytics dataset:

1. **Income vs Spending:** There is a weak negative correlation between annual income and spending score; higher-income customers don't necessarily spend more frequently or score higher on spending metrics. Marketing strategies could emphasize value or loyalty for upper-income segments.

2. **Age Diversity:** Customers are predominantly between 25 and 50, but younger individuals show greater variability in spending behaviors. Targeted campaigns for younger demographics could capitalize on high-spending outliers.

3. **Education and Earnings:** Education level correlates with income, with Masters and PhD holders earning more on average. Education-based segmentation may help in personalized communication or product recommendations.
